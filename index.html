Heracles by mkinsy

Heracles is an open-source complete multicore system written in Verilog. It is fully parameterized and can be reconfigured and synthesized into different topologies and sizes.

Heracles Multicore system, as a part of the Heracles project, can now be found at: http://projects.csail.mit.edu/heracles
